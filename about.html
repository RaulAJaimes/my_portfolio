¿Who is Raúl A. jaimes?


I am Raul Jaimes, retired from the Colombian National Police due to physical disability and I was a student of industrial chemistry, I am disciplined, organized and focused on achievement, discovering my passion for programming, I live in constant training, this is reflected in the section of education.

I have good personal relationships and excellent performance working as a team; In my life I have reached positions as a student representative of the faculty at the Francisco de Paula Santander University and as a police officer I have been in charge of very serious programs, which responded directly to the mayors and governors, managing to stand out for their excellent performance.

I live in Cúcuta – Colombia, a beautiful city on the border with Venezuela, and I have carried out projects to create a text encryption, a hangman game and a basic web page frontend, you can consult this in the section of my projects.

At the moment I have not worked with any company, but I have done autonomous work of exercises of the courses and diplomas I have taken, always with an excellent attitude and great energy, delivering according to established dates.
